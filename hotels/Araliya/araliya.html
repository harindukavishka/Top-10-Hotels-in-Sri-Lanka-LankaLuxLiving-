INTRODUCTION

Araliya Beach Resort & Spa

Located in the stunning coastal town of Unawatuna,
Araliya Beach Resort & Spa is a luxurious beachfront haven that provides the perfect mix of relaxation and adventure. With direct access to the pristine beaches of Sri Lanka’s southern coast, the resort offers an idyllic setting for those seeking a sun-soaked escape. The resort boasts 301 luxurious rooms and suites, all elegantly designed with contemporary aesthetics and modern comforts, with many offering stunning ocean views.



Araliya Beach Resort & Spa is known for its world-class facilities,
including multiple dining options, a sprawling infinity pool, and a state-of-the-art spa that offers a wide range of treatments designed to rejuvenate both body and mind. The resort's culinary experiences are also a highlight, with a variety of restaurants serving everything from authentic Sri Lankan cuisine to international favorites. Guests can also enjoy a range of activities, including water sports, yoga sessions, and excursions to nearby attractions like the historic Galle Fort. Whether you're traveling as a couple, with family, or on a solo adventure, Araliya Beach Resort & Spa is a destination that caters to all.
