INTRODUCTION

Cinnamon Grand Hotel Colombo

Cinnamon Grand Colombo is one of the most iconic luxury hotels in Sri Lanka,
situated in the heart of Colombo’s vibrant city center. This five-star property is known for its grandeur, world-class service, and unparalleled location, making it a favorite among both business and leisure travelers. The hotel offers a wide variety of accommodation options, including elegant rooms and spacious suites that provide a sanctuary of comfort in the bustling city. Each room is beautifully designed with contemporary furnishings and modern amenities.



Cinnamon Grand Colombo is renowned for its diverse dining experiences,
featuring 16 restaurants and bars that offer cuisine from around the world. Whether you're in the mood for authentic Sri Lankan dishes, fine Italian dining, or fresh seafood, you’ll find it here. The hotel also offers a variety of recreational facilities, including two outdoor swimming pools, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a luxury spa. In addition, the hotel’s location provides easy access to some of Colombo’s top attractions, including the National Museum, Gangaramaya Temple, and the bustling shopping districts. Cinnamon Grand is truly a luxurious urban oasis.

Why Choose Us

Nuga Gama – The Village in the City

An authentic Sri Lankan dining experience in a setup that looks like a real village. Traditional clay huts, local cuisine, and live folk music. One of the only hotels in Colombo with this kind of cultural experience.

The Lagoon – Best Seafood Restaurant in Colombo

Known as “Sri Lanka’s best seafood restaurant” by locals and tourists. You can pick your seafood fresh, and the chefs cook it exactly how you want.

Cinnamon Grand Executive Lounge

Exclusive access for business travelers and VIP guests. Offers free snacks, drinks, and private meeting spaces with luxury service.
